Conceptual Overview

This section will introduce the concept of data sonification, developing a framework of vocabulary and ideas for the rest of the toolkit. It will address items such as:

  • What is data sonification?
  • What are the benefits in the context of storytelling and journalism? (emotion + accessibility)
  • How can sonification be applied to journalism?
  • Methods of sonification (e.g. parameter mapping)
  • Musicality, precision, balance between composition and data-pure audio
  • Elements of data and elements of audio synthesis (what are possible audio parameters e.g. timbre, tempo, pitch, volume…) — reference the data + audio map in the data sonification handbook.
  • What is the goal of this toolkit?
